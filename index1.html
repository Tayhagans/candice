About

Candice Adams is a higher education professional who specializes in college admission, diversity recruitment, education equity programs, financial aid, and literacy. She committed to helping students in their pursuit of higher education by assisting them to navigate the college admission process, finding the perfect college fit, and educating students on how to make financially conscious decisions.

Candice currently serves as an Associate Director of Admission for Diversity Programs at the University of the Pacific where she is responsible for helping to propel the University forward in achieving quantity, quality, and selectivity of students while maintaining its commitment to diversity and student success.

With her well-versed background of experience, Candice has succeeded in creating and implementing recruitment strategies while demonstrating a multiplying effect on Pacific’s recruitment plan to promote higher education attainment and support the University of the Pacific's 2020 strategic initiatives.

Candice has a Master’s degree from New York University in Higher Education and Student Affairs along with a Bachelor’s degree from the University of Florida in Telecommunications. In the future, Candice plans to further her career in higher education by becoming a Vice President for Enrollment Management and later a Provost of an institution.

If you would like to know more about Candice, please feel free to connect or send a direct message.
